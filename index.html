About Us

Welcome to Funstone

Founded by Anshita, Funstone Preschool is a nurturing and safe environment focused on the holistic development of children through fun learning methods.

We believe every child is unique and deserves a space where they can explore, create, and grow confidently. Our dedicated team ensures that learning is an adventure every single day!

Playgroup
